About Us

PureJoy Cookies are made with pure, wholesome ingredients and eco-friendly packaging. Our mission is to provide delicious cookies that are good for you and the planet.

Strengths: Healthy and sustainable products, strong brand identity, celebrity endorsement potential, unique selling proposition, quality ingredients.

Opportunities: Expanding market, partnerships and collaborations, product line expansion, online and retail presence, educational campaigns.

Weaknesses: Higher production costs, niche market, brand recognition, supply chain challenges.

Threats: Competition, economic factors, regulatory changes, market saturation, consumer trends.
